## Join in with others

<Steps titleSize={"h3"}>
	<Step title={"Invite the bot"}>
		[https://slotbot.de/invite](https://slotbot.de/invite)
	</Step>
	<Step title={"Enter /add_event_to_channel in a Discord channel of your choice"}>
		To [Add event to channel](/en/integrations/discord/bot-commands/add-event-to-cannel) and view it
	</Step>
	<Step title={"That's it!"}>
		Interested members can register directly using the [/Slot](/en/integrations/discord/bot-commands/slot) command.
	</Step>
</Steps>
_By the way: Any events your community is invited to or you add to your Discord server will also appear directly in the [Event Calendar](/en/events/event-calendar)._

## Creating your own events

<Steps titleSize={"h3"}>
	<Step title={"Invite the bot"}>
		[https://slotbot.de/invite](https://slotbot.de/invite)
	</Step>
	<Step title={"We automatically create a community"}>
		... and add you as an administrator. You will then be able to find the community in the overview at [https://slotbot.de/guilds](https://slotbot.de/guilds) and manage your [Community](/en/communities/configuration) and [members](/en/communities/members) there.
	</Step>
	<Step title={"Open calendar"}>
		You can access your community's calendar from the community overview.
	</Step>
	<Step title={"Create a new event"}>
		You will find the 'Create New Event' button above the calendar.
	</Step>
</Steps>
