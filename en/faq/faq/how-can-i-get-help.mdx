---
title: "How can i get help?"
---

You can ask any kind of question on the Discord: [https://discord.gg/3XU4urPuds](https://discord.gg/3XU4urPuds).

If you have found an error, please try to describe the steps as precisely as possible. Describe what you are doing and what is happening. Also, tell us what you expected to happen instead. Screenshots or videos are always helpful.
