---

Members are the integral part of the communities. Only people listed there have the possibility to register for [reserved slots](/en/events/creating-events/slotlist-rules#reservation).

![Member list of a community](/images/communities/mitglieder/Slotbot-Guild-Members.png)

<Info>
	**Permissions**

	To manage the members a community the `Admin` role is required.
</Info>

New players can be added by [synchronising the member roles](/en/communities/configuration#synchronisation-of-member-roles) or by using the [add community](/en/integrations/discord/bot-commands/add-community) command.
