---
title: "Rename squad"
description: "Renames a squad according to its position in the event. The reserve cannot be renamed."
---

## Command

/rename_squad `squad_position` `squad_name`

## Process

Renames the squad at the specified position in the event to the specified name. The squad counter starts at 0. The reserve cannot be renamed.
